Avg Time: Content A = 10,127, Content B = 0, Delta = -100%

A delta of "-100%" implies Content B is worse. But with 1 pageview and 1 download total between both, these numbers are statistically meaningless. Showing percentage deltas on single-digit data is misleading.

When BOTH assets have very low engagement (total views + downloads < 10 for both), show the performance table but add a prominent note: "Both assets have minimal engagement data (fewer than 10 total interactions each). These numbers are too small to draw reliable conclusions."
